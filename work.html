FIN-Helper

"FIN-Helper" is an AHK GUI designed to perform lengthy iterations of "screen image search" drag and drop routines to attach control points in FIN-lite (a Siemens specific GUI creation software). It was about 25% slower than manual human input BUT - it automated the entire Site UI creation process (no small feat) with zero errors.
